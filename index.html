Mortgage Calculator FAQ

What is a mortgage calculator?

A mortgage calculator is a financial tool that helps you estimate your monthly mortgage payments based on the loan amount, interest rate, and term. It also shows you how much interest you'll pay over the life of the loan.

How accurate is this mortgage calculator?

Our calculator uses industry-standard formulas and is highly accurate for estimation purposes. However, your actual mortgage terms may vary based on your lender, credit score, and other factors.

What's included in the monthly payment calculation?

The basic calculation includes principal and interest. Our comprehensive tool also factors in mortgage insurance (CMHC, FHA, PMI), property taxes, and other homeownership costs for a complete picture.
