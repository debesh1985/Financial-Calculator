Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is this Facebook monetization calculator?

Our Facebook calculator provides estimates based on industry averages and your specific inputs. Actual earnings vary by audience demographics, content quality, and Meta's policies. Check your Creator Studio for precise revenue data.

What are Facebook Stars and how much do they pay?

Facebook Stars are virtual gifts viewers can purchase and send to creators. Each Star is worth $0.01 USD to the creator. Stars can be earned during live streams, video premieres, and regular video content.

How much do Facebook Reels pay creators?

Facebook Reels earnings vary by region and performance, typically ranging from $0.50-$2.00 RPM (per 1,000 views). Payment depends on qualified plays, audience location, and advertiser demand in your market.

What are the requirements for Facebook monetization?

Facebook monetization requires: compliance with Partner Monetization Policies, advertiser-friendly content, original content creation, proper payout setup, and account in good standing. Specific requirements vary by monetization feature.

How do Facebook subscriptions work for creators?

Facebook subscriptions allow fans to pay monthly for exclusive content and perks. Creators typically keep 70% of subscription revenue after platform fees, with rates varying based on purchase method (web vs mobile app).

Can I use this calculator for international creators?

Yes! Our calculator works globally for creators in eligible countries including USA, Canada, UK, Australia, Germany, France, and many others. Results are displayed in USD for universal comparison.

What's the difference between Meta and Facebook monetization?

Meta is the parent company of Facebook. Facebook monetization refers to earning money specifically through Facebook's platform features like Stars, Reels ads, subscriptions, and branded content tools.

How often should I check my Facebook earnings?

Check your actual earnings monthly in Creator Studio ‚Üí Monetization. Use this calculator weekly to estimate potential earnings and optimize your content strategy for better monetization results.
